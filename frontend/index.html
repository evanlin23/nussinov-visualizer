“Inosine pairing weights (I–C, I–U, I–A) were derived from experimental thermodynamic measurements of inosine-containing RNA duplexes (Wright et al., 2007) and scaled to the same framework. Non-canonical or weak pairs (e.g., I–G) were assigned zero weight.”
